Community Activism

At the age of 19, Hussle’s father took both him and his brother on a trip to Eritrea, East Africa for 3 months in 2004. He credits this trip with inspiring him to become a “community activist with an entrepreneurial spirit.

Along with his Vector90 project, Nipsey was involved in the planning and advisory stages of the Destination Crenshaw project which focused on showcasing the history and culture of African Americans in his neighborhood.

In March 2019, Hussle contacted officials from the LAPD to arrange a meeting with him and Roc Nation to discuss what they could do to help prevent gang violence in South Los Angeles. This meeting was scheduled for April 1; it never happened.

Death

Nipsey Hussle was believed to have gotten into an argument with his accused killer, Eric Holder, prior to the fatal incident. Two others were wounded in the shooting. The suspect stood over Hussle and fired the fatal shots before fleeing. All 3 victims were transported to a hospital where Nipsey was pronounced dead.

On April 2, 2019, Holder was arrested and later on indicted on 1 count of murder, 2 counts each of attempted murder and assault with a firearm, and one count possession of a firearm by a felon.

Hussle was laid to rest on April 11 in Forest Lawn Memorial Park in Southern California.

Life After Death

Upon hearing the news of his death, numerous celebrities offered their condolences as well as Los Angeles Mayor Eric Garcetti. A letter from former US president Barack Obama was read during his memorial service.

Following his service, there was a 25.5-mile funeral procession that made its way through the streets of South L.A. including Watts and Crenshaw. Crowds of mourners gathered and lined the streets to demonstrate the impact he had on them.

On the day of his funeral, the intersection of Slauson Avenue and Crenshaw Boulevard was set to be renamed Ermias “Nipsey Hussle” Asghedom Square in his honor.

Within a few months, over 50 murals dedicated to the rapper were painted in the city of Los Angeles as well as all over the country.

Puma released the Marathon Clothing collection in September 2019 with 100% of the net proceeds to the Neighborhood “Nip” Foundation.

A documentary on Nipsey Hussle is in development at Netflix.
