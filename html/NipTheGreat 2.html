The Marathon Continues (2011)

  • Released: November 11, 2011
  • Label: All Money In
  • Format: CD, digital download
  • Peak US Chart #: N/A

    • TMC was Hussle’s sixth official mixtape. He considered this project to be mostly “in-house” and featured few California area guests.

    The tape had guest appearances by Dom Kennedy, YG, Cobby Supreme among others. It also featured production by 1500 or Nothin’, THC, Wizzo and Mistah Mota among others.

    XXL Rating: 4/5 stars.

Crenshaw (2013)

  • Released: October 8, 2013
  • Label: All Money In
  • Format: CD, vinyl, digital download
  • Peak US Chart #: 63

    • Crenshaw was the eigth official mixtape by Nipsey. The first 1,000 copies were released in a limited-edition series for $100 among other incentives thus launching his Proud2Pay movement. Upon its release he completely sold out within 24 hours, 100 of those copies were bought by Jay Z.

    The tape had guest appearances by Rick Ross, Dom Kennedy, Slim Thug, Z-Ro among others. It also featured production by 1500 or Nothin’, Ralo, Wizzo and Jiggy Hendrix among others.

    XXL Rating: 3/5 stars.

Victory Lap (2018)

  • Released: February 16, 2018
  • Label: All Money In, Atlantic
  • Format: CD, LP, CS, Streaming, digital download
  • Peak US Chart #: 2

    • After much delay, Nipsey Hussle released his first and only studio album titled Victory Lap. This was Hussle’s first major commercial release after 13 years of releasing a string of mixtapes. The album debuted at number four on the Billboard 200, selling 53,000 album-equivalent units. It reached its current peak following Nipsey’s death in April 2019. The album also received a nomination for Best Rap Album at the 61st Grammy Awards.

    It had guest appearances by Puff Daddy, Cee-Lo Green, Kendrick Lamar, YG among others. The album also featured production by Nipsey Hussle, Mike & Keys, Murda Beatz, Street Symphony among others.

    XXL Rating: 4.5/5 stars.
